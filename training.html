The Difference

Most AI training teaches tools. This is something different.

Your team is already experimenting with AI. Some of them are using it every day. What most organizations lack is not access to AI — it is the framework for using it well. Without that, speed becomes risk. Enthusiasm becomes liability. And the gap between what AI can do and what your organization should do with it grows wider every quarter.

Dr. Gbemisola Adetayo does not teach your team which buttons to press. She shapes how your organization thinks about AI — its risks, its responsibilities, and what responsible adoption actually looks like in practice. The result is not a workforce that uses more AI. It is a workforce that uses AI better, and an organization that can stand behind how it does.
