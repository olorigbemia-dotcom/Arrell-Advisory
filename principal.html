About Dr. Adetayo

Dr. Gbemisola Adetayo has spent over a decade delivering transformation at every level — from public health technology platforms and global digital commerce to AI governance policy and hands-on business training. She has managed enterprise programs for Lululemon and The Coca-Cola Company, shaped AI policy research at the Center for AI and Digital Policy, and founded three organizations dedicated to responsible AI adoption across different contexts.

She holds a PMP, a Harvard AI Leadership certificate, and certifications in agentic AI systems architecture and AI security governance. She is also the author of two published books on AI safety and career strategy.

She does not theorize about AI adoption. She has delivered it, and now she helps others do the same.
