03 · Priority Actions · 30-Day Window
01
Establish a deployment gate for new AI use cases
No new AI system should go live without a documented owner, use case description, and data classification. This requires no new tooling — it requires a decision that governance is a condition of deployment, not a review after the fact.
02
Conduct a 48-hour AI use case inventory
Identify every AI tool currently in use across your organization. Shadow AI is the most common source of unmanaged governance risk at the Emerging maturity tier.
03
Assign a governance accountability owner
Governance without a named accountable owner defaults to collective responsibility — which in practice means no responsibility. This does not require a new hire. It requires a decision.
